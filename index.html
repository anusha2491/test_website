Renewed Hope FAQs

  • What are your sizes like? Please see our SIZE GUIDE. It's also located in the "size guide" tab of each product page for your convenience. We are generally quite true to size, but if we had to say, we can be on the small side compared with other retailers. However, if you're still unsure about the sizing of particular items, just email us :) We have girls of all sizes in our office, and they would be happy to try something on for you and let you know the fit. The item I want is “sold out” or no longer appears? Email us at info@so.com with the name of the item (or description if you can't think of it) and we'll let you know if we're getting anymore back. If we are, we'll put you on the list to be notified when that particular item comes back in stock. Or, on the sold out products page, you should see a grey box that says "Notify me when my size is back in stock". If you pop your email down, then you'll get an automatic notification when your size is back in stock!
